Engineering Courses

Computer Engineering

Department of Computer Engineering is established in year 2006. The department offers BTech and MTech programs in computer engineering.The department also offers PhD program in Computer Engineering. Since its inception, BTech(CE) program was offered with intake of 60. Present intake is 120.



Civil Engineering

The department offer B.Tech program in Civil Engineering with a yearly intake of 120 students. A group of qualified faculty and adequate experience is the highlight of the department. The department has qualified faculty members occupied with educating and research having perfection in different fields.



Mechanical Engineering

The Department offers undergraduate course in Mechanical engineering and postgraduate courses in Thermal Sciences and Machine Design Specializations. The department has well equipped laboratories attached to different divisions for conducting teaching and research for an intake of 120 students at undergraduate level.
